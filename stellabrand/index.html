Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

TL;DR - Summary

  • We do NOT collect personal data
  • We do NOT track your usage
  • All logo creation happens locally on your device
  • We only use Chrome storage for your preferences
  • Premium payments are handled securely by ExtensionPay/Stripe

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Logo designs or brand content you create
  • Browsing history or activity

Information Stored Locally

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Logo Settings: Your current logo design (text, font, colors, layout)
  • Preferences: Theme preference (dark/light mode), recent colors, recent icons
  • Premium Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription

This data never leaves your device (except premium status sync), is not accessible to us or any third party, and can be cleared by uninstalling the extension.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts API

We connect to Google Fonts to load font files for your logos. Only font data is requested - no personal information is sent.

Google's Privacy Policy

ExtensionPay (Premium Subscriptions)

If you choose to upgrade to Premium, payments are processed by ExtensionPay which uses Stripe.

  • We receive only: subscription status (active/inactive) and subscription date
  • We do NOT receive: payment details, credit card numbers, billing address

ExtensionPay Privacy Policy | Stripe Privacy Policy

Data Security

  • All logo creation and export happens locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers for user data)
  • Premium status is synced securely through ExtensionPay's encrypted channels
  • We use Chrome's secure storage APIs for local data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your stored data via Chrome's extension storage
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export: Download your logos at any time using the export feature

Children's Privacy

StellaBrand does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general audiences.

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Why We Need It
storage Save your logo designs and preferences locally
alarms Schedule premium status checks (for subscription sync)
downloads (optional) Enable one-click logo downloads

Host Permissions

Host Why We Need It
fonts.googleapis.com Load Google Fonts for your logos
fonts.gstatic.com Load font files
www.googleapis.com Access Google Fonts API
extensionpay.com Process premium subscriptions
